1.Monocoptor-

A monocopter or gyropter is a rotorcraft that uses a single rotating blade. The concept is
similar to the whirling helicopter seeds that fall from some trees. The name gyropter is
sometimes applied to monocopters in which the entire aircraft rotates about its center
of mass as it flies. The name "monocopter" has alsobeen applied to the personal jet
pack constructed by Andreas Petzoldt.

2.hybrid vtol-

VTOL Fixed-Wing UAVs combine the benefits of multi-rotor platforms with fixed-wing
drones and transition between the two modes during flight. VTOL fixed-wing drones
are a versatile choice for a wide range of commercial and military aerial applications.
The ability to vertically take-off and land, without the need for a launcher or runway,
means these drones can be operated in almost any location. The fixed-wing design
provides greater endurance, the ability to cover longer distances, and the option to fly
faster, allowing operators to fly for longer and cover more ground when compared to
